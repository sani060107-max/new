Dua 15: Acceptance & Forgiveness

رَبَّنَا تَقَبَّلْ مِنَّا إِنَّكَ أَنتَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ، وَتُبْ عَلَيْنَا إِنَّكَ أَنتَ التَّوَّابُ الرَّحِيمُ

Our Lord, accept (this worship) from us. Truly, You are the All-Hearing, the All-Knowing. And turn to us in mercy. Truly, You are the Most-Forgiving, the Most Merciful.
