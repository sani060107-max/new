Dua 10: At Arafat

خَيْرُ الدُّعَاءِ دُعَاءُ يَوْمِ عَرَفَةَ، وَخَيْرُ مَا قُلْتُ أَنَا وَالنَّبِيُّونَ مِنْ قَبْلِي: لَا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ، لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ، وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ

The best supplication is the one made on the Day of Arafat, and the best of what I and the Prophets before me have said is: "None has the right to be worshipped except Allah, alone, without partner. To Him belongs sovereignty, and to Him belongs praise, and He is over all things competent."
