Dua 5: Before Tawaf

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ، وَصَلَّى اللَّهُ عَلَى رَسُولِ اللَّهِ. اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أُؤْمِنُ بِكَ وَكِتَابِكَ، وَفَوْعْدِكَ، وَأَتْبِعُ سُنَّةَ نَبِيِّكَ مُحَمَّدٍ ﷺ.

In the name of Allah, and all praise is for Allah, and may Allah send blessings upon the Messenger of Allah. O Allah, I believe in You, in Your Book, I am faithful to Your covenant, and I follow the Sunnah of Your Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.
