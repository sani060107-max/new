Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to perform Umrah?

Umrah can be performed any time of the year, but many prefer during Ramadan or off-peak months for comfort.

Is Umrah obligatory?

Unlike Hajj, Umrah is not obligatory but is a highly recommended Sunnah.

Can women perform Umrah without a Mahram?

Regulations vary by country. Some allow it for women above certain ages with official tour groups.
